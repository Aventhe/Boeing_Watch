Introduction - Boeing Watch

Boeing is the world's largest aerospace company and leading manufacturer of commercial jetliners and defense, space and security systems. Boeing products are designed for commercial and military use which include military aircraft, satellites, weapons and defense systems. Boeing has a long tradition of aerospace leadership and innovation. The company continues to expand its product line and services to meet emerging customer needs. Its broad range of capabilities includes creating new, more efficient members of its commercial airplane family, designing, building and integrating military platforms and defense systems. With corporate offices in Chicago, Boeing employs approximately 145,000 people across the globe in more than 65 countries. The company is split in 2 halves: Commerical and Military.

Boeing Watch is an organisation dedicated to bringing users the best and newest information on Boeing aircraft. In this short webpage, we will be exploring the evolution of the most fundamental aircraft that make / have made up the backbone of the world's largest aerospace manufacturer.
