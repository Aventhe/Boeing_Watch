The Boeing Watch Team

The Boeing Watch team comprises of three key individuals: Archit Mahajan, Benedek Horvath and (certainly not) Nicholas Kalapos. These three individuals embarked on a perilous quest to create an interactive mapping system that explored a concept. They chose the intruiging and interesting concept of the evolution of Boeing Aircraft. This was all done in order to complete a preliminary IPT assignment... The survival of this website is entirely dependent on the marks!!!
